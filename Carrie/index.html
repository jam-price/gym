About

Carrie graduated from Birmingham Theatre School and has been touring and performing for the last 10 years. Most recently she has been gallivanting around the country in Shakespearience (Underbelly), a fun, family show of 3 of Shakespeareâ€™s plays in which she plays over 15 characters along with a range of ridiculous accents! The show had a successful run at Edinburgh Festival this year, receiving 4 and 5 star reviews, and has toured to venues including London's Southbank and Leicester Square.

Other credits include The 39 Steps (Guildhall Theatre), Much Ado About Nothing (RSC's The Dell) Alice's Adventures Underground (The Vaults) and Jack and the Beanstalk (Imagine Theatre). Film and TV credits include Flowers (Channel 4) and short film Hark, Villains, which was selected as part of the London Short Film Festival 2017.

Last year she also devised and created her own show in collaboration with Ruffians Theatre, which was part of the first Amplify Festival at Nottingham Playhouse.
